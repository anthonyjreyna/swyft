Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026  ·  Swyft Home Buyers ("Swyft," "we," "us")

This Privacy Policy explains how Swyft Home Buyers collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit www.swyftholdings.com, request a cash offer for your property, or communicate with us. We buy houses directly from homeowners in and around Indianapolis, IN; Cleveland, OH; Columbus, OH; and St. Louis, MO. We keep this simple on purpose: we collect information to prepare your offer and communicate with you about it — and we do not sell your personal information.

1. Information we collect

Information you give us. When you request an offer through our website, a form on Facebook or Instagram, by phone, or by text, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and information about the property — such as its address, condition, occupancy, your selling timeline, your reason for selling, and your price expectations.

Information collected automatically. When you use our website, we and our service providers automatically collect standard technical data such as your IP address, device and browser type, pages viewed, and how you interact with our pages (including through session analytics described in Section 5). If you use our address lookup, mapping data is processed by Google to display your property location.

Information from advertising platforms. If you submit a lead form on a platform like Facebook or Instagram, that platform provides us the information you entered along with basic campaign details (such as which ad you responded to).

2. How we use your information

  • To prepare, deliver, and follow up on your cash offer
  • To contact you about your request by phone call, text message, and email — including through automated technology such as scheduled texts and reminders
  • To manage the purchase process if you sell to us (for example, coordinating with a title or escrow company)
  • To operate, measure, and improve our website and advertising
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements

3. Text messaging (SMS) terms

By submitting your phone number through our website or a lead form, you consent to receive calls and text messages from Swyft Home Buyers about your property and your offer request, including messages sent using automated technology. Consent is not a condition of any purchase or sale.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out of text messages, or HELP for help. You can also reach us at (951) 555-0190 or hello@swyfthomebuyers.com.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging opt-in data and consent are never sold or shared with any third party.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing. We share information only in these limited situations:

  • Service providers who help us operate — such as our customer relationship management platform, telephone and text messaging providers, website hosting, and analytics services. They may use your information only to provide services to us.
  • Transaction partners when you move forward with a sale — such as title companies, escrow officers, and attorneys involved in your closing.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers — if Swyft is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may transfer as part of that transaction, subject to this policy.

5. Cookies and analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors use our site and to measure our advertising:

  • Microsoft Clarity helps us understand how visitors interact with our pages through heatmaps and session analytics, so we can improve the experience.
  • Meta (Facebook) tools, such as the Meta Pixel and Conversions API, help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising and improve who sees our ads.
  • Google services power our address autocomplete and property maps.

You can limit cookies through your browser settings, and you can learn about advertising choices at optout.aboutads.info. Blocking cookies may affect some site features.

6. How long we keep information

We keep your information for as long as needed to respond to your request, complete a transaction, meet legal and tax requirements, and maintain business records. If you ask us to delete your information (Section 8), we will do so unless we're required to retain it.

7. How we protect information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we can't guarantee absolute security — but we work to protect your data and limit access to those who need it.

8. Your choices and rights

  • Texts: reply STOP to any message to opt out.
  • Emails: use the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.
  • Calls: tell us you'd prefer not to be called, and we'll honor it.
  • Access, correction, or deletion: email hello@swyfthomebuyers.com and we'll respond within a reasonable time.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under state privacy laws — such as the right to know what personal information we've collected, to request deletion, and to not be discriminated against for exercising those rights. Because we do not sell or share personal information for cross-context advertising as defined by laws like the California Consumer Privacy Act, no opt-out of sale is necessary — but you may still submit any request using the contact information below.

9. Children

Our website and services are intended for property owners and are not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Third-party links

Our site may link to third-party websites (for example, scheduling or careers tools). Those sites have their own privacy practices, which we don't control — review their policies before providing information.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the effective date at the top of this page. Meaningful changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
